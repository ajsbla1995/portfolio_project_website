Analysis of Wine Scores and Prices

In this project, the relationships of wine scores, prices, and descriptions of over 100,000 wines. The primary goals of this project were to determine:

  • Whether there is a strong correlation between price and scores for varietal wines.
  • The relative score and price for each varietal wine.
  • The frequency of descriptor words used by critics for different price and score segments.
  • The relationship of wine scores vs. price for wine-growing regions in selected countries and visualize.

    • A dataset of wine scores and reviews from a csv file from www.kaggle.com was loaded into and cleaned in MySQL. A database connection between MySQL and Python was then used for further exploratory analysis and visualization in Python. Finally, a Tableau dashboard comparing Old and New World wines based on score and price was built - and can be seen below.

    Analysis of CA Wildfire Seasons (2014-2020)

    This project uses data extracted directly from CALFire.gov to visualize the relative size of major fires California from 2014-2020. I used SQL, Excel and Tableau to explore and visualize the comprehensive state and county statistics of mortality, structural damage, and acreage burned caused by wildfires during this historic period in CA wildfire history.
